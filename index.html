About Excellence Secondary School

Our History

Founded in 1999, Excellence Secondary School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Lagos State for over 25 years. What started as a modest institution with just 50 students has grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in Lagos, with over 1,250 students across JSS and SSS levels.

Our journey has been marked by consistent academic excellence, with our students regularly achieving outstanding results in WAEC, NECO, and gaining admission to top universities in Nigeria and abroad.

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable and accessible secondary education that develops academic excellence, strong moral character, and leadership skills in our students, preparing them to become responsible and productive members of society.

Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in Lagos State, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and producing well-rounded graduates who excel in their chosen fields and contribute positively to nation-building.

Academics

Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3)

Our Junior Secondary curriculum follows the Nigerian Basic Education Curriculum approved by NERDC, covering:

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science & Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Civic Education
  • French Language
  • Yoruba Language
  • Computer Studies
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical & Health Education
  • Home Economics
  • Agricultural Science
  • Christian Religious Studies/Islamic Studies

Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3)

Senior Secondary students choose from three departments:

Sciences

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, English Language, Agricultural Science

Commercial

Commerce, Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, English Language, Mathematics

Arts & Humanities

Literature, Government, History, CRS/IRS, Geography, English Language

Admissions

JSS 1 Requirements

  • Primary 6 Leaving Certificate
  • Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
  • Completed Admission Form
  • Passport Photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Letter of Good Conduct from Previous School
  • Pass Entrance Examination

Age Requirement: 10-12 years

SSS 1 Requirements

  • JSS 3 Certificate
  • Basic Education Certificate (BECE) Result
  • Birth Certificate or Age Declaration
  • Completed Admission Form
  • Passport Photographs (4 copies)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Letter of Good Conduct from Previous School
  • Pass Entrance Examination

Age Requirement: 13-15 years
